Sprouting Hope community garden

The Sprouting Hope community garden provides a 14,000 square foot living classroom for our volunteers to come together and learn how to grow healthy produce. Our hoop houses allow us to produce early and late crops outside the normal gardening season for our area. We maintain bee hives to improve pollination of our crops and provide an annual honey harvest. An edible forest provides fruit and nuts.
